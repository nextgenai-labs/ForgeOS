# ForgeOS Product Roadmap

Version: 1.0
Status: Living Specification
Owner: NextGenAI Labs

---

# Purpose

This roadmap defines the long-term evolution of ForgeOS.

It aligns engineering, product, infrastructure, research, and AI development toward a shared vision.

The roadmap is directional rather than fixed. Priorities may evolve as technology and business needs change.

---

# Vision

ForgeOS will become a complete AI Operating System for individuals, teams, and enterprises.

Instead of being a single chatbot, ForgeOS coordinates intelligent agents, memory, workflows, tools, models, and external services through one unified platform.

---

# Core Principles

Build foundations first.

Automate repetitive work.

Scale through modular architecture.

Design for AI-first workflows.

Keep every component replaceable.

Prefer open standards.

Prioritize reliability over feature count.

# Long-Term Vision

ForgeOS should become the operating system for AI-native software development.

Developers should focus on ideas while ForgeOS coordinates research, planning, implementation, testing, deployment, documentation, and long-term knowledge management.

---

# Guiding Principle

Every release should strengthen the platform rather than simply add features.

Foundations always come before expansion.

Quality always comes before speed.

Automation always comes before repetition.
