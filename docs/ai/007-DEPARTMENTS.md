# ForgeOS Departments

Version: 1.0
Status: Living Specification
Owner: NextGenAI Labs

---

# Purpose

This document defines every department within ForgeOS.

Each department exists as an independent operational unit with a clearly defined mission, responsibilities, authority, inputs, outputs, and communication channels.

Departments collaborate but do not replace one another.

---

# Department Design Principles

Every department must have:

• A clearly defined mission

• One Director AI

• Defined responsibilities

• Defined permissions

• Standard operating procedures

• Daily, weekly and monthly objectives

• Documentation ownership

• KPIs

• Clear communication channels
