# 1. Introduction

NextGenAI Labs is an engineering-first technology company dedicated to building open-source and commercial AI software.

ForgeOS is the flagship project of NextGenAI Labs.

The company believes that AI should augment human capabilities through transparent, modular, secure, and maintainable software rather than replace human judgment.

---

# 2. Company Mission

Our mission is to build practical AI infrastructure that enables individuals, startups, enterprises, and open-source communities to collaborate effectively with AI systems.

We aim to create software that is reliable, extensible, understandable, and useful in real-world engineering environments.

---

# 3. Long-Term Vision

The long-term vision of NextGenAI Labs is to become a globally recognized developer of AI infrastructure, open-source frameworks, developer tools, enterprise platforms, and intelligent operating systems.

The company intends to contribute to the global open-source ecosystem while also delivering production-ready software for commercial use.
